ChitChat

ChitChat is a simple chat application that helps you connect with other people around the world.

In today's digital world it can be difficult to view and exchange information anonymously. ChitChat is an open-source project intended to enable such without compromising security or privacy. Since this is just a fun side project of mine, there are no mysterious third parties analyzing and collecting user activities :)

Meet new people!

Search and select a chat room to join without any lengthy sign-up forms that ask for your personal information.

Create your own chat rooms!

Can't find your own chat room? Just create your own! Enhance and tailor chat rooms to your needs by setting passwords, hiding them, or making them completely open for everyone.

(Coming soon) End-to-end encryption!

In addition to standard TLS support, private and secret chat rooms will allow users to securely transmit data through end-to-end encryption of payloads from sender to receiver. As a result, only the people communicating can read the messages and absolutely no other person, even if the server itself gets compromised.
